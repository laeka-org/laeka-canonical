# Matrix 3 -- Audience

**Scope**: sixty-four registers of reader or listener. For each: survival (PASS), degradation (PARTIAL), or collapse (FAIL) of the current formulation, plus reformulation proposal for FAILs.

**Structure**: eight axes times eight levels each = 64 cells.

**Rubric**:
- **PASS**: current formulation intelligible, credible, and resistant in this register.
- **PARTIAL**: intelligible but loses impact, risks misreading, or needs glossary.
- **FAIL**: incomprehensible, counterproductive, or generates predictable backlash in this register. Reformulation required.

**Pass criterion**: a substantial majority (at minimum 50/64 cells) must be PASS or PARTIAL. A claim with FAIL in more than 25% of registers is too fragile for the proposed publication format.
