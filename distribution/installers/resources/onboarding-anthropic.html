Laeka Setup — Step 1 of 3

Create Your Anthropic Account

You need an Anthropic account with a Claude subscription to use Laeka.

  • 1
    Go to Anthropic.com Click the button below to open the signup page.
  • 2
    Create a free account Enter your email, verify it, and set a password.
  • 3
    Choose a Claude subscription Claude Pro or Max gives you the full Laeka experience.
  • 4
    Return to the installer dialog Once subscribed, go back to the setup wizard and click OK.

Open the Anthropic signup page in your browser:

Go to Anthropic.com Manage Subscription

Choose a plan:

Claude Pro
$20 / month
Good for light daily use
Claude Max
$100–$200 / month

Once subscribed, return to the setup dialog on your screen and click OK to continue. This page will close automatically when setup is complete.
